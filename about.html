About

I’m Rihash, a frontend developer with a growing passion for web technologies and digital creativity. My journey into coding started with curiosity—writing my first lines of HTML and CSS just to see how websites work—and it quickly turned into something much bigger: a real interest in building meaningful, functional, and beautiful web experiences. Over time, I’ve built a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’m constantly exploring advanced concepts, frameworks, and design practices. What excites me most about frontend development is the balance between logic and creativity. I love the process of turning a simple idea or design into an interactive, responsive website that people can actually use and enjoy. When I’m not coding, I like to learn new tools, refine my problem-solving skills, and explore trends in technology and design. I believe that continuous learning is key in this field, and I’m eager to grow not just as a developer but as a creator who can bring value to projects and people. In the future, I aspire to expand into full-stack development, work on larger-scale projects, and contribute to solutions that improve the way people interact with technology. For me, every project—big or small—is a chance to learn, improve, and leave a lasting impact.
